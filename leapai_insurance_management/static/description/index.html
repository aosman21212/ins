How It Works

Four steps from policy creation to claim settlement

1

Create Policy

Select category, term, agent and policyholder. Add insured person details and nominees. Set premium and payment type.

2

Confirm & Invoice

Confirm the policy to lock terms. Generate a premium invoice for the policyholder. Mark Running once payment is received.

3

Manage Claims

Policyholder raises a claim with reason and documents. Submit for manager approval or rejection. Record settlement amount.

4

Settle & Report

Approve the claim and generate a settlement bill. Print PDF reports for audit. Renew the policy when it expires.
